Sound of Rain Association is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit music organization based in the Seattle area, dedicated to promoting Chinese-American cultural exchange through live music, performances, and community engagement.

Through contemporary Chinese pop, rock, and crossover music, we aim to connect cultures, bring communities together, and create meaningful musical experiences.
